2018 · Earth-1610
Into the Spider-Verse

Miles Morales is bitten and must step up when Peter Parker falls. He meets Gwen, Peter B. Parker, and a multiverse of web-slingers who teach him what it means to be Spider-Man.

2023 · Multiverse
Across the Spider-Verse

Gwen opens a portal to Miles' world. Their reunion sparks a chain of events that puts Miles at odds with the entire Spider-Society. He refuses to let a canon event take his father.

Beyond the Spider-Verse

Miles is trapped in Earth-42 — a universe without a Spider-Man — while Gwen assembles a team. The story of their connection, separated by dimensions, races toward its conclusion.
